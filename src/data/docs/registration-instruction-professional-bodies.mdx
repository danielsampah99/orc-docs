# Registration Instructions

## Overview

The **Registration Instructions** page provides users with a clear and concise overview of the process involved in registering a professional body. It outlines the required steps to ensure a smooth and accurate application.

![Registration Overview|2000X1238](/img-professional-bodies/pb-img-2.png)

## Registration Steps

![Acknowledgement and Proceed|2000X1238](/img-professional-bodies/pb-img-3.png)

- Carefully read through each of the instructions provided on the page.
- Ensure you understand the eligibility criteria and the required documentation.
- Prepare all necessary information before beginning the registration process.


After reviewing all the steps, you must check the **"I have read and understood"** checkbox to confirm your acknowledgement.

Then, click the **Proceed** button to continue with the registration.
