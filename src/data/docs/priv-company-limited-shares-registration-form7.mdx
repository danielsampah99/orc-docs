In this final section, users are given the option to upload any supporting documents that may be required or relevant to the registration process.

## Uploading Attachments:

###  Step : Click the "Click or Drag and Drop" area to upload files.

This will open your file explorer to select documents, OR

###  Step :You can drag files directly into the upload zone.
 

### ** Note:** Supported document types include:

- PDF

- DOC/DOCX

- PNG/JPG (for scanned forms or ID)

Uploaded files will be listed for review. You can:

Delete unwanted files

Download to preview

### ** Note:** You may skip this step if you do not have any additional documents to attach.

##   Step : Once all relevant files are uploaded, click "Submit" to Procced to the Review Page.
